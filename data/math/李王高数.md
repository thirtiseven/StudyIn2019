### 1**inf

凑基本极限 $$\lim[1+\varphi(x)]^{\frac{1}{\varphi(x)}} = e$$，其中 $$\lim \varphi(x) = 0, \varphi(x) \neq 0 $$

改写成指数/取对数洛必达

结论：$$\lim \alpha(x)=0,lim \beta(x) = \infty$$，且$$\lim \alpha(x) \beta(x) = A$$，则
$$
\lim [1+\alpha(x)]^{\beta(x)} = e^{A}
$$
